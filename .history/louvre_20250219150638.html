Le Musée du Louvre The Louvre Museum

The Louvre, originally a royal palace and now the world's most visited museum, houses over 380,000 objects and displays 35,000 works of art, including the iconic Mona Lisa. Its glass pyramid entrance, designed by I.M. Pei, beautifully blends contemporary architecture with classical French Renaissance style.
