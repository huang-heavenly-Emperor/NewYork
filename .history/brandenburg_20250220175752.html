Brandenburger Tor Brandenburg Gate

The Brandenburg Gate, completed in 1791, stands as Berlin's most iconic landmark. This neoclassical monument has witnessed Germany's pivotal moments, from Napoleon's conquest to the fall of the Berlin Wall. Today, it symbolizes German unity and European peace, its majestic columns and Quadriga sculpture drawing millions of visitors to experience its timeless grandeur.
