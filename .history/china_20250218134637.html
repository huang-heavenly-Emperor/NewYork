中国 China

Beijing, China

The Great Wall

Best Time: Mar-May, Sep-Oct Walking Time: 2-4 hours

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Great Wall stretches over 13,000 miles across China's northern borders. Built over 2,000 years ago, this magnificent structure served as both a military defense and border control.

Highlights

  • Stunning mountain vistas
  • Photography spots
  • Ancient watchtowers
Historical Site Adventure
Central Beijing

The Forbidden City

Visit Duration: 3-4 hours Built: 1406-1420

The world's largest palace complex, spanning 72 hectares with 980 buildings. This UNESCO World Heritage site served as the imperial palace for 24 emperors during the Ming and Qing dynasties, showcasing the pinnacle of Chinese architectural achievement.

Highlights

  • Imperial throne halls
  • Royal treasures
  • Ancient artworks
Imperial Palace Museum
Huangshan, Anhui

Yellow Mountain

Best Season: Spring/Autumn Height: 1,864m

Renowned for its "Four Wonders": peculiar pine trees, spectacular granite peaks, hot springs, and the mystical sea of clouds. Yellow Mountain has inspired Chinese art and literature for millennia with its breathtaking landscapes.

Highlights

  • Sea of clouds
  • Ancient pine trees
  • Natural hot springs
Natural Wonder Scenic Area
Xi'an, Shaanxi

The Terracotta Army

Visit Duration: 4-5 hours Built: 210-209 BC
