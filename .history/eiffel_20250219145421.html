Tour Eiffel Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, an engineering marvel and symbol of Paris, has captivated visitors with its elegant iron lattice design since 1889. Standing at 324 meters, it offers breathtaking views of the City of Light.

History

Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, the Eiffel Tower was designed by Gustave Eiffel and his team of engineers. Initially meant to be temporary, it became a permanent icon of Paris.

Architecture

The tower's unique iron lattice design combines both engineering precision and artistic beauty, using 18,000 iron parts held together by 2.5 million rivets.

Statistics

  • Height: 324 meters
  • Weight: 10,100 tons
  • Steps to top: 1,665
  • Annual visitors: 7 million+

Experience

  • Panoramic views of Paris
  • Gourmet restaurants
  • Evening light shows
  • Champagne bar at the top
