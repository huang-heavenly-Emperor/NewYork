Big Ben

00:00

Big Ben, the iconic clock tower of the Palace of Westminster, stands as a majestic symbol of London and British democracy. Completed in 1859, this masterpiece of Gothic architecture rises 316 feet into the London skyline. Its four massive clock faces, each 23 feet in diameter, have kept time for Londoners for over 160 years, while its distinctive chimes have become one of the most recognizable sounds in the world.
