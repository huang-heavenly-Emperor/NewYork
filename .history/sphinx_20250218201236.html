أبو الهول The Great Sphinx

The Great Sphinx of Giza, an ancient marvel carved from a single limestone ridge, has stood guard over the Giza plateau for over 4,500 years, embodying the wisdom and mystery of ancient Egyptian civilization.

Ancient Guardian

A Monument of Mystery and Power

Carved from the bedrock of the Giza plateau, the Great Sphinx is one of the most enigmatic monuments of ancient Egypt. With the head of a human and the body of a lion, it embodies the strength and wisdom of the pharaohs.

Height: 20 meters
Length: 73 meters
Age: ~4,500 years
The profile of the Sphinx reveals its majestic proportions

Architectural Marvel

Carved from a single limestone ridge, the Sphinx demonstrates the incredible architectural capabilities of ancient Egyptians.

Historical Significance

Believed to represent Pharaoh Khafre, the Sphinx served as both a guardian of the pyramids and a symbol of royal power.

Mythological Symbol

The Sphinx combines human intelligence with leonine strength, embodying the perfect union of mind and power.
