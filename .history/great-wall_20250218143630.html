World's Longest Wall

Stretching over 13,000 miles, the Great Wall of China stands as humanity's largest ancient architectural feat. Built across centuries by various Chinese dynasties, it served both as a military fortification and as a means of border control, regulating trade along the Silk Road.

  • Cable car available
