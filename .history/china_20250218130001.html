中国 China The Middle Kingdom

China, in East Asia, is the world's most populous nation with a 5,000-year history. Known for the Great Wall, Terracotta Army, and diverse cuisine, it's a global economic leader. Its landscapes range from the Himalayas to megacities like Beijing and Shanghai. Festivals like Lunar New Year highlight its rich culture.

Beijing

Great Wall

世界七大奇迹之一，横跨中国北部的宏伟建筑。蜿蜒于崇山峻岭之间，见证了数千年的历史变迁。

Beijing

Forbidden City

世界上最大的宫殿建筑群，中国明清两代的皇家宫殿。精美的建筑艺术和丰富的文化遗产令人叹为观止。

Yellow Mountain

中国最著名的山岳之一，以奇松、怪石、云海、温泉闻名于世。云雾缭绕间展现出独特的东方美学。
