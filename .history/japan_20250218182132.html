Shizuoka, Japan

Mount Fuji

Best Time: Jul-Aug Height: 3,776m Peak Season: Summer

Japan's highest mountain and most iconic symbol, Mount Fuji is a perfectly shaped volcano that has inspired artists and poets for centuries. Its majestic silhouette is recognized worldwide as a symbol of Japan.

Highlights

  • Breathtaking sunrise views from the summit
  • Sacred Shinto shrines along climbing routes
  • Stunning reflection in surrounding lakes
  • Popular climbing destination in summer

Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto Temples

Best Time: Mar-May, Oct-Nov 1600+ Temples Cherry Blossom Season

Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan, is home to over 1,600 Buddhist temples and Shinto shrines. The city's historic districts, traditional gardens, and ancient architecture transport visitors back in time to old Japan.

Highlights

  • Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji)
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine's torii gates
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Traditional tea ceremonies
Modern Icon City View Night Scenery
Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo Tower

Best Time: Evening Height: 333m Observation Decks

Tokyo Tower stands as a symbol of Japan's post-war rebirth and economic miracle. This communications and observation tower offers spectacular views of the Tokyo skyline and, on clear days, Mount Fuji in the distance.

Highlights

  • Stunning night illumination
  • 360-degree city views
  • Special light-up events
  • Top Deck Tour experience
