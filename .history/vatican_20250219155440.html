Città del Vaticano Vatican City

Vatican City, the world's smallest sovereign state and the spiritual center of the Roman Catholic Church, is a treasure trove of art and history. Home to St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums, it houses some of humanity's greatest artistic achievements, including Michelangelo's frescoes and countless masterpieces collected by popes through the centuries. This independent city-state, established in 1929, continues to be a powerful symbol of faith, art, and cultural heritage.
