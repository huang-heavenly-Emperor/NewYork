Schloss Neuschwanstein Neuschwanstein Castle

Perched atop a rugged hill in Bavaria, Neuschwanstein Castle is the epitome of Romantic architecture and a testament to King Ludwig II's artistic vision. Built in the 19th century, this fairy-tale castle, with its soaring towers and dramatic setting, inspired Walt Disney's Sleeping Beauty Castle and continues to enchant millions of visitors with its magical blend of fantasy and architectural brilliance.
