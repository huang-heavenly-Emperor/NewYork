Tower Bridge

Bridge Status: Closed

Tower Bridge, completed in 1894, is a combined bascule and suspension bridge that has become one of London's most iconic landmarks. This masterpiece of Victorian engineering allows tall ships to pass through its lifting central spans while maintaining a vital river crossing. Its distinctive blue and white color scheme, Gothic towers, and high-level walkways with glass floors offer visitors a unique perspective of London, the Thames, and the bridge's fascinating mechanical heart.
