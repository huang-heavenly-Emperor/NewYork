Colosseo The Colosseum

The Colosseum, an iconic symbol of Imperial Rome and human architectural achievement, stands as the largest ancient amphitheater ever built. This magnificent structure, completed in 80 AD, once hosted gladiatorial contests, dramatic performances, and other spectacles for up to 50,000 spectators. Today, it continues to inspire awe as one of the world's most remarkable monuments to ancient Roman engineering and culture.
