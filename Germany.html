Bavaria

Neuschwanstein Castle

Built 1869 Romantic style Royal Palace

Neuschwanstein Castle, a 19th-century Romanesque Revival palace, sits majestically atop a rugged hill in Bavaria. This fairy-tale castle inspired Disney's Sleeping Beauty Castle.
